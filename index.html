    • A Little Bit About Us
    Ever since 1985, the goal of this clinic has always been to provide kind and quality care at a fair price. Dr. Antonio Solomon was driven by his passion to support the welfare of animals and established this clinic over 33 years ago. Today, we continue to live by his ideology. In 2018, the clinic was acquired by the RSP Veterinary Corporation owned by a group of experienced veterinarians. They intend to keep the clinic the same as it has always been and continue Dr. Solomon's mission in providing quality and affordable health care.
